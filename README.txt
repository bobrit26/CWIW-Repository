The files can be broadly separated into 4 groups:

A - CDC_..., NCES_..., US_... - data is the original data as taken from their sources, no changes

B - regression_... .csv, student_..., suicides_..., tax_..., total_... - data is various stages of modified data as reproducible in the cleaning script

C - regression_results.docx, script_1 and script_2 - are the final results of the analysis

D - suicides_... .pdf, Suicide-... .png - are the auxiliary tables and plots accompanying the regression
